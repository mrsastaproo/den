Common questions

Quick answers

Is DEN free?

Yes. DEN is completely free to download and use. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no paywalls.

Does DEN require an account?

No. You can download, install, and use DEN immediately — no email, no sign-up, no account of any kind.

Is the Windows version available?

Not yet — it's actively in development. Android is available now. We'll announce the Windows version when it's ready.

Is it safe to install an APK?

When downloaded directly from this official website, yes. Always verify the URL before downloading. We don't use third-party distribution.
