Privacy Policy

How DEN handles information — from our website and our app.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

DEN ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DEN website and the DEN mobile application (collectively referred to as the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your information.

We have written this policy to be clear and straightforward. DEN is designed with privacy as a foundational principle. We collect as little information as possible and use it only for the purposes described below.

By accessing our website or downloading and using the DEN application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is genuinely necessary to provide or improve the Service. The types of information we may collect fall into two categories: information you provide voluntarily, and information that is collected automatically when you interact with our website.

Information you provide voluntarily

If you contact us via our contact form, email, or any other means of communication, we may collect:

  • Your name (if provided)
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing lists or marketing campaigns without your explicit consent.

Information we do not collect

DEN does not require user accounts. The application does not prompt you to register, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app. All data generated by your use of the app is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your communications: When you contact us, we use the information you provide to reply to your message and resolve your query.
  • To improve the Service: Aggregated, anonymized data about how visitors interact with our website (if collected via analytics tools) may be used to understand usage patterns and improve the website's design and content.
  • To comply with legal obligations: In limited circumstances, we may be required to retain or disclose information to comply with applicable law or a valid legal process.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Device and Usage Data

The DEN application is designed to operate entirely on your device. It does not transmit usage data, crash reports, telemetry, or any other behavioral information to our servers or any third-party services by default.

Any content you create or manage within the app is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the application, this locally stored data may be removed from your device in accordance with your operating system's behavior.

If we introduce optional features in the future that involve data transmission (such as cloud sync), those features will be clearly disclosed, optional, and governed by separate terms communicated to you at that time.

5. Downloads and App Distribution

DEN is distributed as an Android APK file via our official website. When you visit our website to download the application, our web server may log standard server access data, which can include:

  • Your IP address
  • The date and time of your visit
  • The specific file or page requested
  • Your browser type and operating system
  • The referring page (if any)

This information is collected automatically by standard web server software as part of operating the website. It is used for security monitoring, abuse prevention, and general operational purposes. We do not use this data to identify individual users or build behavioral profiles.

Server logs are retained for a limited period and are not shared with third parties except where required by law or to address security incidents.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar browser storage mechanisms to function correctly. These are limited to technically necessary operations such as remembering your preferences within a single browsing session.

We do not use cookies for advertising targeting, cross-site tracking, or to serve personalized content based on your browsing behavior across the internet.

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality of our website but will not affect your ability to download the application.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services in limited and disclosed capacities. These may include:

  • Web fonts: We may use externally hosted fonts (such as Google Fonts), which may result in your browser making a request to a third-party server. This is limited to font delivery and does not involve sharing any personal information about you.
  • Hosting providers: Our website is hosted on a third-party server. Our hosting provider may process technical access data in the ordinary course of providing hosting services.

We carefully evaluate any third-party services we use and aim to minimise the number of third parties that have any interaction with our users. We do not embed third-party advertising, social media tracking pixels, or marketing analytics tools on our website or within our application.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services that you may navigate to via links on our website.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate technical measures to protect information collected through our website from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include using encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all web traffic.

However, no method of transmission over the internet, and no method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect any information we hold, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Since the DEN application stores all user data locally on your device, the security of your in-app data is also dependent on your device's own security practices, such as using a screen lock and keeping your operating system updated.

9. Children's Privacy

DEN is a general-purpose application and website not specifically directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately using the details in Section 12 below. We will promptly review and, where appropriate, delete any such information.

10. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights in relation to any personal information we hold about you. These may include the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information we hold about you
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent where our processing is based on consent

Given that DEN collects very limited personal information — primarily through contact messages — we can typically fulfil these requests quickly. Please contact us using the details in Section 12 to make any such request. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

We do not make any specific compliance certifications (such as GDPR certification) that we cannot substantiate, but we aim to act in the spirit of these frameworks and treat your data with care and respect.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the Service evolves or as legal requirements change. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the Service following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you disagree with the changes, please stop using the Service and contact us if you have specific concerns.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at:

Email: support@denmusic.in

We will make every reasonable effort to respond to your inquiry in a timely manner.
