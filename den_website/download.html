How to install DEN on Android

1

Download the APK

Tap the "Download APK" button above. The file will download to your device's storage — usually in your Downloads folder.

2

Allow installation from unknown sources

Since DEN is not distributed via the Play Store, Android will ask if you want to allow installation from this source. Tap Settings and enable it for your browser or file manager.

3

Open the APK file

Open your file manager, navigate to the downloaded APK, and tap it to begin installation. Follow the standard Android install prompts.

4

Open DEN

Once installed, find DEN in your app drawer and launch it. No sign-up required. No permissions you don't expect. Just open and go.
